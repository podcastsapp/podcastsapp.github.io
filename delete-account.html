Delete Your Podcasts. Account

If you would like to delete your Podcasts. account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Email us at podcastsapp.business@gmail.com with the subject line: "Delete my account".
  2. We will delete all associated data, including:
    • Firebase login information
    • Firestore data (user settings, saved playlists, etc.)
    • App reviews
  3. Data will be removed within 7 days of your request.

Thank you for using Podcasts.
